## 4. Opportunity Summary
- **The "Local Dominance" Play**: By targeting district-specific terms (Wakiso, Luweero) and the specific school name (God's Grace Primary), KCM can rank #1 for niche terms faster than trying to compete globally for "Child Sponsorship."
- **The "Trust" Play**: Targeting transactional terms with informational guides (e.g., "How much to sponsor a child?") captures donors at the research phase.
